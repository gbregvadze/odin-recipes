Pasta

Description

Carbonara is a pasta dish made
with eggs, hard cheese, fatty cured pork, and black pepper

Ingredients

  • 100g pancetta
  • 50g pecorino cheese
  • 3 large eggs
  • 350g spaghetti
  • 50g unsalted butter

Steps

  1. Put a large saucepan of water on to boil.
  2. Finely chop the 100g pancetta, having first removed any rind.
    Finely grate 50g pecorino cheese and 50g parmesan and mix them together.
  3. Beat the 3 large eggs in a medium bowl and season with a little freshly grated black pepper.
    Set everything aside
  4. Add 1 tsp salt to the boiling water, add 350g spaghetti and when the water comes back to the boil, cook at a constant simmer,
    covered, for 10 minutes or until al dente (just cooked).
  5. Squash 2 peeled plump garlic cloves with the blade of a knife, just to bruise it.
  6. While the spaghetti is cooking, fry the pancetta with the garlic.
    Drop 50g unsalted butter into a large frying pan or wok and, as soon as the butter has melted,
    tip in the pancetta and garlic.
  7. take the pan of spaghetti and pancetta off the heat.
    Now quickly pour in the eggs and cheese. Using the tongs or a long fork, lift up the spaghetti so it mixes easily with the egg mixture, which thickens but doesnâ€™t scramble,
    and everything is coated.
